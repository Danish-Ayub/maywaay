global and local shipping service

My Waay provides fast shipping solutions locally and globally to all regions and cities of Saudi Arabia and to more than 200 countries around the world within 2-4 shipping days with attention to the sent materials and delivery with great commitment from the point of receipt to delivery by our dedicated team.

Free liquid food

The My Waay team with its high experience facilitates the shipment of liquid materials and various food products such as honey or Zamzam water and others, and packaging them completely in a competent manner by specialists to maintain their quality and efficiency under different shipping conditions until they reach the specified destination free of damage.

Shipping of dangerous materials by professionals

The My Waay team and its highly experienced team provide a service for shipping hazardous materials such as perfumes, batteries, etc., where they are packaged and shipped with the help of specialists and experienced people in the My Way team.

Safe packing for shipment

To avoid any delays that may be caused by the method of packaging the shipment, My Way provides the correct and safe packaging procedures for the shipment before sending it to ensure that there are no obstacles that may hinder the arrival of the shipment on time or its retrieval.

My Waay offers a shopping service from Saudi Arabia to provide what the student needs outside the Kingdom of consumable materials or food products, assembling and packaging them, and then shipping them to the recipient’s door wherever he is.
